About CollegeTips.in & Pet-Friendly City

Who We Are

CollegeTips.in is India's largest and smartest student community, dedicated to empowering youth with knowledge, opportunities, and fun campaigns. Our Pet-Friendly City initiative aims to transform Indian cities into safe, welcoming spaces for pets and their humans.

Our Mission

  • Promote pet-friendly policies and infrastructure
  • Educate communities about responsible pet ownership
  • Encourage volunteering and community participation
  • Share inspiring pet stories and create lasting impact
